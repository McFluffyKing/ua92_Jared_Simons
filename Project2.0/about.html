About Us

Our Company

A year ago our company blasted off with an idea that we keep on believing to this day: the need for a space where music-lovers can find all their favorites in one sitting. We make sure that the music you buy from us is not only authentic, but continuously growing. As we believe, one small step for man, one giant leap for music-kind.

Our Future

The Sound Space Station's has a vision for our future were we will continue to expand our music catalouge, along with collaborating with new and up-coming artists and producers to keep providing our users with the best possible music experience out there! Along with this we also plan to expand our store to provide music equipment and accesories to ensure we become the one-stop-shop for everything music!
